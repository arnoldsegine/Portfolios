TravelBuddy AI

An original AI product concept: from problem discovery to technical vision.

✈ TravelBuddy AI
"Travel that understands you."

AI-powered identity-aware travel discovery that helps travelers find culturally relevant, faith-aware, and identity-specific services anywhere in the world.

The Problem

Generic results don't serve everyone.

Through independent user research, I identified a clear market gap: platforms like Google Maps and Yelp return generic results with no understanding of identity-specific travel needs — culture, faith, language, grooming, or diaspora services. A Black traveler in Copenhagen shouldn't scroll through 40 irrelevant barbers. A Muslim traveler in Florence deserves to find a hijabi-friendly salon without guesswork.

The Solution

Natural language AI that understands context.

TravelBuddy AI uses LLM-powered natural language search to interpret traveler identity context and return relevant, culturally-tagged recommendations. The AI parses location, service type, identity needs, cultural context, and language preferences — then surfaces the right businesses.

User Flow

Four steps to feeling seen.

1

2

AI interprets location, identity, and service context

3

4
